Features

Secure

We employ cutting-edge encryption and state‑of‑the‑art smart contract security measures. Every transaction and recovery process is audited and monitored in real time.

Private

Your data remains confidential with our decentralized storage solution. Your secret phrases and recovery data are encrypted and only accessible to you.

Decentralized

With no single point of failure, our system leverages a network of trusted guardians. Your wallet recovery isn't dependent on any central authority.

Time-Locked

Our built‑in timelock features add an extra layer of security. A mandatory waiting period is enforced before any changes can be executed.
